About Our Service

How It Works

Our AI-powered skin disease prediction system uses advanced deep learning technology to analyze images of skin conditions. The system is built on MobileNetV2, a lightweight but powerful neural network architecture that can efficiently process images while maintaining high accuracy.

Important Notice

This tool is for educational and preliminary screening purposes only. It is not intended to replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions.

Features

  • Quick and easy image upload
  • Instant analysis results
  • Privacy-focused design
  • Multiple skin condition detection

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. All images uploaded to our service are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers. The analysis is performed locally, and no personal information is collected or stored.
